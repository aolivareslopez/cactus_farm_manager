# cactus_farm_manager

The Coachella Valley faces serious water scarcity, food insecurity, and economic inequality especially in cities like Coachella, Mecca, and Thermal. Nopal cactus is one of the most water-efficient crops possible in a desert climate, culturally familiar to the valley's majority Latino population, and highly profitable across multiple revenue streams.
This app gives small cactus farmers the data visibility that larger operations take for granted.
